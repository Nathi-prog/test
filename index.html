Hand Works Auto Detailing Offers

Washing the car

Car washing is a very serious matter. Depending on the selected option, I will wash your car standardly, in detail or in a detailing manner. If your car is very dirty and has impurities in the form of tar, pollen and insects, you have come to the right place. The "two-bucket" washing technique, active foam, decontamination and degreasing - these and other procedures, I will perform on your car as part of the wash. Of course, I will wash and degrease the windows so that they remain clean for a long time.

Interior

This beautiful interior of the car makes you want to come back to it. The seats, the headliner, plastic elements are exposed to dirt and mechanical damage every day. Velvet, Alcantara or leather? I can handle each of these materials. Thanks to professional detergents, preservatives and protection agents, I will restore the factory shine, preserve and protect the upholstery so that it remains resistant to dirt for a long time. From now on, the view of the interior of your car will delight you!

Rims and wheels

"A good rim" is essential. Every expert on the subject knows this. As part of the services related to aluminum rims, I offer cleaning with the application of protective wax as well as renovation with painting in the selected color. Steel rims? No problem. Cleaning, renovation with painting are the treatments that I will perform for you. To complete the effect, it is also worth taking care of the tires. I will wash and apply a protective coating that will give the tires a pleasant shine. The cherry on the cake will be a detailed washing of the wheel arches.
